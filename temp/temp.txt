### description

With the success of the Data Platform  (SQLSaturday) event on 01st FEB 2020 and to continue the community spirit _the Edinburgh Data Platform user group_ would like to convene another major event on 03rd June 2023. 
<br>
**_Data Saturday Edinburgh_** is a free-to-attend '_**in-person'**_ event (one day) for IT professionals who are keen on data platform technologies. This is a community-led event, that offers useful educational & career-supported content across the data platform, cloud computing, analytics, architecture and much more.
