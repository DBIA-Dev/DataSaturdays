Data Saturday #3 Haiti
Mar 27 2021 - Virtual

English Version

Are you looking for a way to advance your career using the latest technologies? Are you looking to learn the latest Data Platform & Analytics solutions? Then, Haitian SQL One Day Event is your answer. This community is for professionals who use the Microsoft data platform. This event offers contents across data management, cloud and hybrid architecture, Data Engineering, Data Sciences, DataOps, AIOps, Big Data Pipeline, IoT, AI, ML, DevOps, Scala, Python, Business Intelligence, Data Visualization & Dashboards, Docker, Kubernetes & Cloud Native Infrastructure, SQL Database, and more.

It will be hosted online on March 27th 2021 between 8:30am to 5:30pm in three (3) different languages (Creole, French, and English). Please register to reserve your seat; also don’t forget to submit your session(s).

Creole Version

Ansèyman sa-a ap pase an liy jou kap 27 Mas 2021 an, soti 8:30 nan maten pou rive 5:30 nan aprèmidi, nan 3 lang diferan: Kreyòl, Fransè ak Anglè. Tanpri, enskri pou ou ka rezève plas ou e sitou, pa bliye voye pwogram ou si'w ta renmen prezante youn oubyen plizyè sesyon.
